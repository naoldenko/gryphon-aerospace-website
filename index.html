ABOUT US

GAC was founded in the Fall of 2020 by a group of dedicated mechanical engineers. The club was subsequently reorganized in the Fall of 2022 and has grown significantly since, with over 20 active members, 3 subteams, all to compete in the AEAC competition! The aerospace club will soon grow to include a rocketry team!
